My Story

I'm Yesudasu Vemula, a ESE (Electronics and Communication Engineering) student at SRK Institute of Technology, Vijayawada, and a passionate national-level badminton player. My journey is a unique blend of academic excellence and sports achievements. I have consistently balanced my technical pursuits with a deep commitment to professional badminton. In the world of sports, I am a **State Gold Medalist** and have proudly represented Andhra Pradesh and India in several prestigious tournaments. I won the **All India Inter-SAI (Sports Authority of India) U19 Singles Bronze Medal**, **U19 Doubles Bronze Medal**, and **Men’s Doubles Bronze Medal**, demonstrating my strength in both individual and team competitions. I have also represented **JNTU Kakinada University** at the university level and was a **semifinalist in the AP State Championship**. These experiences have instilled in me qualities of discipline, resilience, and strategic thinking. On the academic side, I am deeply interested in **software development, web technologies, and system design**. I am learning and working on technologies like HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Node.js, and MongoDB. I enjoy building real-world projects that solve meaningful problems, and I aim to grow into a skilled **full-stack developer** and tech innovator. I believe in giving my 100%—whether it’s a fast-paced badminton match or writing clean, efficient code. I’m always open to new challenges, learning opportunities, and collaborations that help me grow as a **developer, athlete, and leader**.
